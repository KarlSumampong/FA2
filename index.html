GreenSense

Smart Insights for Small Greenhouse Farmers.

Key Features

1. Transaction Tracking Every time a farmer buys something or sells a crop, GreenSense records it. You can log purchases and sales through a mobile phone or computer.
2. AI Insights GreenSense analyzes the data automatically. It looks at all your expenses, sales, and crop performance to calculate profit per crop, cost per plant, and seasonal trends.
3. Alerts and Suggestions The platform can send alerts if a crop isn’t performing well, if costs are higher than usual, or if a particular crop is very profitable and should be planted more.

Why Choose Us?

Many tools for farmers are either too complicated or designed for large industrial operations. Spreadsheet tracking is manual and time-consuming. Traditional farm management software is expensive and often too complex for small-scale growers..
GreenSense is different because it is:
• Designed for small farms - Easy to use and affordable.
• Actionable - Focuses on insights farmers can actually use, not just raw data.
• Smart but simple - Uses AI to analyze trends without requiring farmers to understand complex math or technology.
